📝 Real IELTS Prompt — Cambridge 16, Test 4
"In the future, driverless vehicles will be used to transport people and goods. Some people believe that the advantages of driverless vehicles outweigh the disadvantages. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"

Prompt Deconstruction

ComponentAnalysis
TopicDriverless vehicles used for transport of people and goods
Task TypeAdvantages/Disadvantages — "Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?"
Key Verbsoutweigh, agree, disagree
ScopePresent AND future implications
RequirementTake a clear position (agree / disagree / partially agree)

⚠️ Common Pitfall

Do NOT write about whether driverless vehicles will exist. The debate is whether advantages outweigh disadvantages. The first sentence is context, not the debate question.

💬 Cohesive Devices Bank

Advantages

  • One compelling argument in favour of… is that…
  • A key benefit lies in…
  • Proponents point out that…

Counter-arguments

  • That said, there are legitimate concerns regarding…
  • Critics contend that…
  • Opponents argue that…

Rebuttal

  • Nevertheless, this concern can be mitigated by…
  • While this is a valid point, it fails to account for…
  • This drawback is overshadowed by the fact that…

"Outweigh" Language

  • …outweigh the drawbacks
  • …tip the balance in favour of…
  • On balance, the benefits are more significant than…
