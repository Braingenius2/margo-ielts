PEEL Structure:

Point: "I firmly believe that..."
Explanation: "The reason for this is that..."
Example: "A clear illustration of this would be..."
Link back: "Therefore, it seems evident that..."
1. Tourism Impact: "In what ways does mass tourism affect local communities, both positively and negatively?"
2. Preservation vs. Development: "Some people believe that cities should prioritize preserving historical buildings, while others argue that modern development is more important. What is your view?"

Task 2: Writing Response (150–200 words)

Write a 150–200 word response to this prompt:
"To what extent do you agree that preserving historical landmarks is more important than building modern infrastructure? Give reasons and examples."

Structure Aid (PEEL):
Paragraph 1: State your position clearly
Paragraph 2: First reason + example (PEEL)
Paragraph 3: Second reason / counterargument + example
Paragraph 4: Conclusion — restate position

  • Use formal cohesive devices: It is widely believed that…, From a societal perspective…
